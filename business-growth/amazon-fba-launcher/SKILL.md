# Amazon FBA Product Launcher

## Purpose
Strategic skill for planning and executing Amazon FBA product launches from India to the USA marketplace. Provides end-to-end business blueprints covering market analysis, product design, manufacturing, export compliance, Amazon listing optimization, and launch strategy.

## When to Use
- Planning a new physical product launch on Amazon USA
- Evaluating product-market fit for Amazon FBA
- Building cost models for cross-border e-commerce
- Designing differentiation strategies for commodity product categories

## Assets
- `assets/indian-chakraa-business-blueprint.md` — Complete 14-section consulting blueprint for a premium chakra crystal tree + spiritual card deck product launch

## Blueprint Sections
1. Amazon Market Analysis — competitive landscape, pricing, negative review analysis
2. Product Design — specifications, bill of materials, durability engineering
3. Card Deck System — value-add companion product design
4. Premium Packaging — gift-worthy unboxing experience design
5. Manufacturer Sourcing — India/China supplier evaluation framework
6. Cost Structure — unit economics, landed cost, profitability model
7. Export from India — IEC, GST, HS codes, freight options
8. US Compliance — CPSIA, Prop 65, labeling requirements
9. Trademark & Brand Registry — USPTO filing, Amazon Brand Registry enrollment
10. Amazon Listing Strategy — optimized title, bullets, image strategy, A+ content
11. Differentiation Strategy — competitive moats, gift psychology
12. Launch Strategy — PPC structure, review generation, influencer seeding
13. Execution Roadmap — 24-week week-by-week plan
14. Risk Analysis — risk matrix with mitigation strategies

## Key Frameworks
- **Negative Review Mining** — Systematic analysis of competitor complaints to design superior products
- **Double-Lock QC System** — Mechanical + adhesive attachment for fragile components
- **Gift Psychology Matrix** — Triggers that drive gift purchase decisions
- **DDP Export Model** — Simplified export approach for first-time Indian exporters
